LET'S TALK ABOUT AEON FITNESS

What We Do
Our team at AEON Fitness has over 30 years of industry expertise, with each project and client relationship is held to the highest standards. Whether you’re purchasing a simple fitness equipment to work out at home, planning for a facility, or outfitting a professional fitness center, we provide solutions for every fitness level, budget and lifestyle.
Our Story
For several decades, Aeon Fitness has been dedicated to creating fitness solutions that benefit both facilities and users. Our aim is to be your wellness partner for life.
80’s
How we began
AEON FITNESS was founded in Los Angeles in 1981 and was established a treadmill spare parts factory in Taiwan in 1990s. We supply tremendous running decks and running belts of treadmill to some well-known fitness’s factories. Our international R&D centers provide enhanced quality and more comprehensive technology, encompassing all aspects of fitness equipment development.
00’s
Innovation
We use the innovative technique to make outstanding fitness equipment. In 2010, we invested in robotic machine and Enterprise Resource Planning System to improve our production process to be more effective.
20’s
Better than better
We continue to develop distinguished fitness equipment to ensure our users have the best training experience. Our goal is to provide a more active, inspired and comfortable workout experience for our clients.
